Is Cybele Smart Lite perfect for you?

Cybele Smart Lite is a powerful, non-invasive fleet management solution featuring the AI dashcam, a High Definition recorder with up to 3 channels, GPS and 4G for real-time vehicle surveillance. Designed for mini busses, minivans, bakkies and light motor vehicles. The solution includes Advanced Driver-Assistance System (ADAS) , Driver Status Monitor (DSM) for enhanced safety. The compact design features a hidden antenna and GPS module for optimal signal, while supporting up to 1TB of SD card storage. Additional features include an emergency alarm button, a built-in accelerometer and gyroscope for detecting reckless driving events, . Cybele Smart Lite is built to ensure reliable and efficient fleet oversight .
