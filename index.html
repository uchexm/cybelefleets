Key Features
Driving
Safer Fleets

At Cybele Fleet, we leverage AI technology to ensure a safer, more efficient fleet, providing real-time insights and proactive safety measures to protect drivers and enhance operational security.

Advanced Driver Assistance
System (ADAS)

Advanced Driver Assistance System
(ADAS) uses sensors and cameras to
monitor road conditions, providing
alerts for hazards, lane departures,
and collisions. This technology
enhances driver safety, reduces
accident risks, and supports safer,
more efficient journeys.

Driver Monitoring System (DMS)

Driver Monitoring System (DMS) uses
cameras and sensors to detect driver
fatigue, distraction, and unsafe
behaviour, alerting them in real time.
This technology helps prevent
accidents, promotes safety, and
ensures drivers remain attentive on
the road.

Our Safety Models

Driver Behaviour Vehicle Operation G-Force Events

Phone Usage

Detects when the driver’s attention shifts to their phone screen, diverting their eyes from the road, and triggering an alert..

Fatigue Detection

Detects drowsiness, suggesting breaks to prevent accidents.

Distracted Driving

Detects Distracted Driving and alerts driver to stay focused on the road

Phone Calling

Detects if the driver is making a phone call, triggering an alert to minimize distractions and improve safety.

Smoking Usage

Detects if the driver is smoking, issuing an alert when prohibited or when carrying flammable cargo due to safety hazards

Driver Recognition

Identifying who is operating the vehicle, authorised driver or not. Linking the below events to the authorised driver.

Lane Deviation

Alerts the driver when unintentionally drifting out of the lane, helping maintain safe positioning

Pedestrian
Collision Warning

Detects pedestrians ahead and issues an alert to prevent potential collisions.

Forward
Collision Warning

Warns the driver of vehicles or obstacles ahead, especially if approaching too quickly, to reduce collision risk.

Following Distance

Alerts the driver when following too closely to maintain a safe distance from the vehicle ahead.

Harsh Braking

Detects sudden, forceful braking and issues an alert to encourage smoother driving.

Harsh Acceleration

Identifies rapid acceleration, signaling the need for gradual speed increase to maintain control.

Harsh
Cornering

Monitors sharp, highspeed turns that could affect vehicle stability and safety.

Speeding

Alerts the driver when exceeding the speed limit, promoting safer, regulated driving.
