Is Cybele Smart Essential perfect for you?

Cybele Smart Essential is a robust fleet management solution tailored for trucks and heavy-duty vehicles, featuring an AI-enabled MDVR, a High Definition recorder with up to 8 channels, GPS, and 4G for realtime vehicle surveillance. Designed for trucks, tankers, and buses, the solution includes Advanced DriverAssistance System (ADAS), Driver Status Monitor (DSM), and Blind Spot Detection (BSD) for comprehensive safety enhancements.


The system supports up to 4TB of HDD storage with an optional SD card backup, along with advanced features like anti-vibration design, automatic heating for extreme cold, and a wide voltage power supply. Cybele Smart Essential is built to deliver dependable and efficient fleet management for the most demanding operations.
